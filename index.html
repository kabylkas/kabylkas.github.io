Research

I am working at the best lab in the universe -- Micro Architecture Santa Cruz (MASC). I work under the supervision of the best professor in the universe -- Jose Renau.

Currently, my research is headed towards hardware verification. Verification is a hard problem and current industry practices for verifying hardware are bad! A few questions that I am trying to answer:

is there a better metric to measure quality of the verification?
how can we find bugs more efficiently?
is there more productive way of verifying processors?
